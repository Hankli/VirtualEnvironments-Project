Before a level starts, leave both hand controllers docked on the centerpiece's indented surface.
If hand controllers were properly docked, it should prompt the you to press the start button. 
If hand controllers were not docked, ensure they are situated comfortably in your hands before doing anything else
Follow the sequence of steps as prompted by a text box that appears in the upper center of the screen
Press the left controller's trigger button first 
Press the right controller's trigger button second
Press start button
